# Project Overview

> Generated by setup wizard from PRD.

## What It Is

LaunchCast is an automated daily podcast that scrapes Product Hunt's trending launches, deep-dives into each product's actual website using Firecrawl, and delivers an engaging two-host conversation covering today's launches — who they're for, what's actually good, and what's just hype. "Product Hunt, but you just listen."

## The Problem

Indie hackers and vibe coders want to stay on top of daily Product Hunt launches but don't have time to scroll through listings, click through to each product site, and evaluate what's real vs hype. Current alternatives are surface-level summaries (miss the depth) or manual browsing (takes too long). There's no passive, audio-first way to get meaningful daily PH intelligence.

## The Solution

An end-to-end automated pipeline that:
1. Scrapes Product Hunt's daily top launches via Firecrawl
2. Deep-dives each product's actual website for real features, pricing, and audience
3. Generates a natural podcast script with two AI hosts who debate each product
4. Synthesizes high-quality audio via ElevenLabs
5. Delivers via a web player with show notes, product links, and deep-dive modals

## The Hosts

- **Aero** (SKEPTIC) — Tears down hype to find real engineering value. Dry, analytical.
- **Nova** (OPTIMIST) — Sees how new tech empowers creators and reshapes markets. Warm, upbeat.

Each host gives independent numeric ratings (1-10). Disagreements are highlighted as the most engaging moments.

## The User

### Primary: Vibe Coder
Solo developer or indie hacker shipping side projects. No time to browse PH daily, misses launches relevant to their stack. Listens during commute or while coding.

### Secondary: Startup Founder
Early-stage founder monitoring the competitive landscape. Needs quick competitive intel without context switching.

### Tertiary: Tech Curious
PM, designer, or tech enthusiast who follows trends. Wants a passive, audio-first way to stay informed.

## Differentiator

- **Deep website analysis** — doesn't just parrot the PH blurb, actually visits and analyzes each product's real website
- **Two-host debate format** — Aero (skeptic) vs Nova (optimist) makes it engaging, not just informational
- **Live generation visibility** — users watch the episode being built in real-time (Firecrawl URLs streaming in)
- **Interactive mode** — users can voice-chat with the hosts to ask follow-up questions
